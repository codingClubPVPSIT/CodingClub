Welcome to Coding Club

Organised by CSE(AI&ML) & CSE(DS) | Powered by PVPSIT

Learn, Build, and Grow with us in the world of technology.

Our Club Goals

Empowering students through coding, creativity, and collaboration — all within immersive escape room experiences.

Our Mission

To foster creative problem-solving, logical reasoning, and collaborative coding skills by designing immersive and challenging digital escape room experiences.

Our Vision

To cultivate an innovative community where students explore programming, storytelling, and puzzle design in a gamified setting—enhancing both technical skills and critical thinking.

Faculty Advisor's Message

"It is with great excitement that we launch the AI Club at PVPSIT. This initiative marks a significant step towards building a vibrant community for aspiring AI enthusiasts. I am confident that this club will become a hub of innovation, where students can explore, learn, and collaborate on the technologies that are shaping our future. I encourage all curious minds to join us in building this community from the ground up and to embark on this exciting journey together."

Building Strong Coders with Problem Solving & Logic Skills.

Become a part of the Coding Club—and let coding fuel your curiosity and creativity! Here, everyone gets a chance to shine: tackle exciting challenges, develop sharp problem-solving skills, and build up your programming confidence in a friendly, supporting environment. Whether you’re a novice or an aspiring coder, our club helps you unlock your potential and have fun while you learn, solve, and grow together.
